About me


Im a software developer, mainly focused towards Javascript and React but also have experience with Java and Golang. I like creating games and enjoy creating new useful programs for myself, participating hackatons and getting small dopamine hits by solving problems.


I like collaboration with other people, I enjoy planning out a project and dividing it into different parts. If my ultimate coding team was a football club, I would be a midfielder like Xavi - I take pride in being a good team player, passing a ball to others and scoring goals as a team. In my mind it's very important to understand not everyone can be Messi's or Ronaldo's, somebody needs to defend and pass the ball to them.


In my free time, I like to do sports, go for hiking with the dog. I also enjoy boardgames and doing/listening music.
